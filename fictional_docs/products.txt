Zynthora Technologies Product Line

1. Lumina-Core Series
The flagship product line featuring bio-synthetic processors. The Lumina-Core X9 operates at 800 zettaflops, while the newer X12 model achieves 1.4 yottaflops. These chips use engineered chloroplast matrices to convert light energy directly into computational power. Price range: 12,000 to 45,000 galactic credits.

2. Resonance Drives
Introduced in 2858, these storage devices use harmonic vibrations in crystallized dark matter to store information at densities of 900 yottabytes per cubic centimeter. The drives are completely immune to electromagnetic interference and can operate in vacuum conditions. Available in sizes from 50 yottabytes to 8 zetabytes.

3. Phase-Shift Networking Modules
Created by Sylas Moonridge, these modules allow instantaneous data transmission across distances up to 200 light-years by exploiting quantum entanglement in specially grown communication crystals. Each module requires calibration with Zynthora's proprietary Temporal Sync Protocol.

4. Neural-Link Interfaces (Project Meridian)
Still in beta testing as of 2863, these interfaces allow direct brain-computer interaction using non-invasive resonance fields. Early trials show thought-to-code conversion speeds of 12,000 operations per second with 94% accuracy.

5. Helix Quantum Arrays
Inherited from the Helix Dynamics acquisition, these modular computing arrays can be configured in formations of 8 to 10,000 nodes, creating distributed processing networks capable of solving previously impossible mathematical problems.
