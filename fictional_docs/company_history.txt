History of Zynthora Technologies

Zynthora Technologies was founded in 2847 by Dr. Mara Velkanos and Engineer Jax Thornwell in the floating city of Nebulon-7. The company started as a small workshop specializing in quantum-crystalline processors that could operate at temperatures exceeding 4000 kelvin.

In 2851, Zynthora released their breakthrough product: the Lumina-Core X9, a bio-synthetic computing chip that used photosynthetic enzymes to process data. This revolutionary device could perform 800 zettaflops while consuming only ambient light as power.

The Great Merge of 2855 saw Zynthora acquire their competitor, Helix Dynamics, for 47 billion galactic credits. This acquisition brought the legendary engineer Sylas Moonridge into the company, who would later invent the Phase-Shift Algorithm.

By 2860, Zynthora had grown to employ over 250,000 workers across 12 planetary systems. Their headquarters moved to the crystal spires of Arcturus Prime, where gravity fluctuations allowed for better quantum coherence in their manufacturing processes.

The company motto "Innovation Through Resonance" was coined by CEO Velkanos during the annual Convergence Summit of 2862, where she unveiled the company's 50-year vision for neural-integrated computation.
