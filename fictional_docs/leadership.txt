Zynthora Technologies Leadership Team

Dr. Mara Velkanos - Founder & Chief Executive Officer
Born on Kepler-442b in 2815, Dr. Velkanos earned her doctorate in Quantum Biophysics from the University of Titan at age 23. She pioneered the theoretical framework for photosynthetic computing while working at the Andromeda Research Institute. Known for her 19-hour workdays and her pet phosphorescent dragon, Shimmer. She speaks 7 languages including Ancient Martian.

Jax Thornwell - Co-Founder & Chief Technology Officer
Originally a shipwright from the mining colonies of Ceres, Thornwell taught himself advanced engineering by reverse-engineering derelict alien spacecraft. His innovation in thermal-resistant materials made the Lumina-Core possible. He famously works from a zero-gravity workshop and has never attended a board meeting in person, preferring holographic attendance.

Sylas Moonridge - Chief Innovation Officer
Joined Zynthora during the 2855 Helix Dynamics acquisition. Holder of 847 patents across 23 planetary systems. Creator of the Phase-Shift Algorithm and the Temporal Sync Protocol. Known for his eccentric habit of conducting experiments during solar storms. Has a collection of 2,000 antique mechanical calculators.

Dr. Zenna Quill - Chief Scientific Officer
Former professor of Xenobiology at Europa Institute. Specialized in bio-synthetic interfaces and organic computing substrates. Led the development of the Neural-Link Interface project. She maintains a research lab aboard the orbital station Prometheus-9 where micro-gravity conditions enhance cellular growth experiments.

Admiral Koss Vander - Chief Security Officer
Retired from the Interplanetary Defense Fleet after 30 years of service. Responsible for protecting Zynthora's quantum encryption systems and trade secrets. Implemented the seven-layer security protocol that makes Zynthora's facilities the most secure in the galaxy. Expert in both digital and physical security systems.
