Zynthora Technologies Facilities

Headquarters - Arcturus Prime
The main campus spans 400 square kilometers in the Crystalline Valley of Arcturus Prime. The facility uses gravity fluctuations naturally occurring in the region to enhance quantum coherence in manufacturing. The campus includes 47 research buildings, 12 manufacturing plants, and the famous Resonance Gardens where experimental bio-crystals are grown. Employee count: 89,000.

Research & Development - Prometheus-9 Orbital Station
Located in stable orbit around Jupiter's moon Europa. The station's micro-gravity environment is essential for growing the organic computing substrates used in Neural-Link Interfaces. Houses 4,200 researchers and 800 support staff. The station rotates at 0.3 RPM to provide slight artificial gravity in living quarters.

Manufacturing Complex - Titan Industrial Zone
Built into the hydrocarbon lakes of Titan in 2854. Produces 80% of all Lumina-Core processors and Resonance Drives. The facility employs 125,000 workers and operates 24 solar hours per day across three shifts. Notable for its completely automated crystal growth chambers that span 2 kilometers underground.

Advanced Computing Lab - Nebulon-7
The original workshop where Zynthora began. Now serves as an experimental facility for high-risk prototyping. The floating city's unique electromagnetic fields allow testing of devices that would be impossible to study on planetary surfaces. Staff of 3,000 specialized engineers and scientists.

Data Center - Ceres Underground Complex
Buried 50 kilometers beneath Ceres' surface, this facility houses the largest collection of Helix Quantum Arrays in known space. The complex processes data for 14,000 client organizations across multiple star systems. Protected by Admiral Vander's seven-layer security system and naturally shielded by dense metallic ore deposits.

Sales & Support Hub - Mars Dome Alpha
Customer-facing facility in the Mars Dome Alpha colony. Handles client consultations, training programs, and technical support for all Zynthora products. Features a full-scale demonstration center where clients can test products before purchase. Team of 8,000 sales engineers and support specialists.
